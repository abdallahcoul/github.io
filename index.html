Welcome to My World

Mali, officially the Republic of Mali, is a landlocked country in West Africa, covering approximately 1,240,192 square kilometers. It is bordered by Algeria to the north, Niger to the east, and several other countries to the south and west. The capital city is Bamako. Historically significant, Mali was once home to powerful empires, notably the Mali Empire, which thrived from the 13th to 16th centuries and was a major center for trade and Islamic culture. Timbuktu, a city within Mali, became renowned as a hub of learning and commerce.
