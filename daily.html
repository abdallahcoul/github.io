Family and Community

Families and community play a significant role in daily life in Mali, with many people living in extended family units.

Work and Education

Agriculture is a major part of life, while education is increasingly prioritized for young children in urban and rural areas. The fruit and vegetables produced in the farms are sold at market (Sougou).
